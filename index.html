⚡ THE GOVERNING RULE — Manual Departures Only
Flights do NOT auto-turn-around. You must manually depart each plane from every airport. Exception: layover/stopover routes auto-continue through the stopover (one continuous dispatch). Since you log in 2×/day, 12hrs apart, you can trigger only 2 departures/day per plane — one per login.

Consequence: the ideal flight lands just before your next login (~11:45) so you dispatch it again immediately — no idle time, no missed window. A route showing "3–4 trips/day" in the bot is a trap: you can only fly 2, so the plane sits idle. This is exactly why long stopover routes win — one dispatch flies through 2 legs (~11,800km) covering maximum distance/revenue in the single dispatch you get per window.
